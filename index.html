Research Areas

Exploring the frontiers of autonomous robotics through innovative approaches to perception, estimation, and control in challenging environments.

Space-Based Perception

Developing robust visual and sensor-based perception systems for spacecraft navigation in extreme environments with limited lighting conditions.

Learn More

Generative Estimation

Creating probabilistic frameworks for state estimation that leverage generative models to handle uncertainty in space robotics applications.

Learn More

Multi-Robot Systems

Researching distributed robot teams for collaborative planetary exploration, with robust communication protocols for harsh environments.
